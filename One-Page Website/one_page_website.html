    History

    Joes soup began in 2003 when Joe had some friends over for dinner one night. He made a carrot soup that blew his friends away. Joe was out of work at the time and his friends joked that he should open a soup company. Well that joked manifested itself into a real idea and now almost 20 years later Joe has a whole line of soup and ships across the continental US!
